# Hezbollah Second Front

Hezbollah agreed to a ceasefire with Israel in November 2024 after suffering significant losses. However, the assassination of Khamenei — their ultimate patron — and the ongoing destruction of Iran may push them to re-enter the war out of ideological obligation or strategic calculation.

## Injection

- **Israel** must now fight on two fronts. Hezbollah's 100,000+ rocket arsenal threatens Israeli population centers. This could force Israel to divert resources from the Iran campaign and potentially trigger a ground operation into southern Lebanon.
- **Iran** gains a critical ally opening a second front, relieving pressure on the homeland. But Hezbollah's entry risks bringing the entire Levant into the war.
- **US** must decide whether to support Israeli operations in Lebanon, further stretching military resources and political capital.
- **Market** should treat this as a major escalation — regional war expansion triggers panic buying in energy and gold.
